# Outcomes and Significance

The expected outcome is selection without erasure. A timing perturbation should change which representation becomes dominant during retrieval while leaving the competing representation available under other conditions. The result would mean that behavioral commitment reflects the selected pattern, not the total internal state of the system.

The significance is the separation between presence and influence. A representation can remain biologically available while another representation guides action. A person can miss something, recognize something, or carry something while another state organizes the next step. The mechanism does not explain the person. The mechanism explains why presence alone is not the same as behavioral control.

The separation between presence and influence is the part I needed language for. When you said, "it's not like I don't miss you," I heard the possibility that something was present without becoming the selected pattern. I am not using that sentence to prove what was happening inside you. I am using it to name the structure I was trying to understand: something can be present and still not guide action.

The medication structure belongs in the same frame. Weekly appointments and limited access seemed to create continuity around the state-changing input. Access without the same structure seemed to make the present easier to organize around older pain, even while the life we were building was still there. The claim is not that the structure explains the outcome. The claim is narrower: structured access and unstructured access can change the stability of the retrieval state.

The human meaning remains unresolved. I am not trying to prove you wrong or turn the mechanism into an argument for reversal. I am trying to understand how something real could still exist and still not be enough to hold the present in place. Not erasing what we had does not mean keeping us tied together. It means recognizing that what happened mattered, even if another selected state carried behavior somewhere else.
